The Purple Standard — Core Meaning

“The Purple Standard” represents Arion’s pursuit of engineering perfection, design excellence, and professional discipline. It’s the team’s internal and external promise — that every car, every part, every interaction reflects a higher benchmark of quality and innovation.

Purple, as Arion’s signature color, carries dual meaning:

  • Prestige and creativity — showing that Arion aims for mastery with originality.
  • Unity and purpose — blending the passion of red and the calm control of blue, symbolizing balance between speed and precision, creativity and discipline, engineering and artistry.

So, The Purple Standard is our declaration:

“We set the standard in STEM Racing.”
