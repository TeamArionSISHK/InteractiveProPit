The Purple Standard: Core Meaning

The Purple Standard represents Team Arion’s commitment to engineering perfection, design excellence, and professional discipline. It stands as our promise: that every car, every part, and every presentation embodies our pursuit of innovation, quality, and integrity.

Purple, our defining color which symbolizes dual excellence:

  • Prestige and creativity: reflecting our drive to achieve mastery through originality and innovation.
  • Unity and purpose: merging passion and precision, balancing artistic vision with engineering discipline.
“We are not just another STEM Racing team, we set the standard.”
